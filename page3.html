Day 1

Arrival at Delhi

Add ons Added

Half Day Tour

You will arrive at Delhi airport and our representative will welcome you in the Indian traditional way by flower garlanding & tilak on your forehead as in India we say 'Atithi Devo Bhava' which means 'The guest is equivalent to God'.

Flower garlanding are generally offered as a mark of respect and honor. They are offered to welcome the visitors or in honor to the Gods and Goddesses.

Tilak is a ritual mark on the forehead. It can be put in many forms as a sign of blessing, greeting, or auspiciousness. The Tilak is usually made out of a red vermilion paste (Kumkum) which is a mixture of turmeric, alum, iodine, camphor, etc.

You will be transferred to the hotel. Check-in hotel. Overnight: Delhi
